About Nouros

From there my fascinating journey towards engineering began. It was in 2005 when I get to have a computer for the first time. That abacus could run games like Virtual Cop 2, Hercules, DX ball, and whatnot. Playing around with the box for more than 8 years I sailed my ship in 2013 towards computer science. I started to learn about the witchery of the computer. Till now I can say I am still learning, and I am glad that this journey is a never-ending one.

Also, I have a keen interest in cybersecurity as well.
